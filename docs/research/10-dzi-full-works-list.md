# Complete DZI BEL Literary Works Reference

## Overview

There are TWO distinct programs relevant to the DZI exam:

1. **OLD PROGRAM (pre-2022, used 2007-2021)**: 15 authors, ~80 works total across all of them. No thematic modules -- works were studied as part of the traditional literature curriculum.

2. **NEW PROGRAM (2022 onwards, current)**: 9 thematic modules (тематични кръгове), 27 works in the MANDATORY part (задължителна подготовка), split between 11 клас and 12 клас. Plus an extensive "profiled preparation" (профилирана подготовка) module with additional works.

The DZI exam **currently** tests the 27 mandatory works organized by thematic modules. However, the full ~80 works from the old program remain the canonical literature corpus that students are expected to know.
